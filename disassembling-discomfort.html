Disassembling Discomfort 2018
Scroll to view the entire drawing →

A drawing roll exploring the discomfort that comes with the unknown. Each frame zooms in on the previous image, revealing the layers of persistent complexity that accompany our pursuit to understand life. It starts with observational sketches from our starting point, St Pauls Church, merging from abstract image to the next until re-forming into a comforting whole, more rational but still with many mysteries.
