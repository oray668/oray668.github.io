A tarp borrowed from a friend was dirty after being used to cover a boat. We threw it over the fence to clean dirt from it, and discovered the beauty of this light, semi-transparent layer between us and the sky. We secured it using concrete blocks for an afternoon to shade us from the harsh sun. In 2028, we replaced and extended part of the roof and finished it in a similarly light way.
